    Ajoutée le 24 avr. 2014

    If you are looking for thumbnails for your image or video slider, you do not need another WordPress plugin. With Soliloquy, you only need to install the Thumbnails addon.

    This video teaches you how to add thumbnail images to the best responsive slider in WordPress — Soliloquy.

    If you have a Developer's License with Soliloquy, you can install the Thumbnails addon and have thumbnails displaying with your slider in just a few minutes. The Thumbnails addon expands the functionality of the standard Soliloquy plugin by adding a tab to the slider set-up menu.

    The Thumbnails addon is customizable, allowing you to set the dimensions of the thumbnail images, margin, number of images shown, and more. Your thumbnails will highlight the image that is being displayed in the slider, allowing for clear and simple navigation between images.

    How have some of our customers already used the Soliloquy plugin with thumbnails?

    -- REAL ESTATE — Displaying a main image while previewing additional properties or views of the same property.
    -- PHOTOGRAPHY — Showcasing the highlights from a wedding as a website header.
    -- BLOGGER — Highlighting key activities from recent vacations and travels.
    -- BUSINESS — Creating a Featured Content slider, where they display customer testimonials.

    The addition of the thumbnails provides a subtle stimulus to the reader to check out how they have succeeded in various markets.
